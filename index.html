Schedule A Trip

Reserve a 6 or 8-hour fishing charter between April 20th and December 15th.

Call Captain Don at 240-876-6079 or Schedule A Trip Online!

Evening cruises and other special trips can be negotiated; contact the Captain for more details.

Walk On

Board the Hot Pursuit, docked at the Rod ‘N’ Reel Marina (4160 Mears Ave, Chesapeake Beach, MD) and Anchor Yacht Basin (1048 Turkey Point Rd, Edgewater, MD). Capt. Don will confirm departure location upon booking.

All you need to bring is your hat, sunglasses, food, and drink. We will provide you with all of the licenses and gear that you will need. Responsible alcohol consumption is permitted. Smoking is allowed in outdoor spaces on the vessel.

If you are interested in hotel accommodations at the marina, be sure to check with Capt. Don directly as we are able to offer you a discounted rate at Chesapeake Beach Resort and Spa.
