Terms
$650 (6-hour trip) and $800 (8-hour trip) are the minimum base fares. They cover the fares for 6 passengers. These prices will not be adjusted for groups of fewer than 6 passengers. $80/person is added to the base fare for each person over 6. (Please note that there is an 8 person minimum for Saturday and Sunday morning charters). Customers are responsible for organizing their group, and there will only be one group on the boat per trip. The mate works for tips and a 20% tip is customary. Recommended Group Size: 8-12 people. Larger groups are welcome. Fish cleaning charges depend on the type and number of fish we catch. For example, cleaning charge is $5/fish for large rockfish and $2.50/fish for smaller rockfish. Bottom fishing trips may incur an additional bait charge.
Evening Cruises:
Enjoy an afternoon or evening cruise on the Chesapeake. We can make arrangements to transport you to a restaurant or local bar. Another option is to have the event catered with your favorite food and drink. For up to 10 people: $300 for 2 hours, $450 for 3 hours, $600 for 4 hours. Additional passengers are $30 per person (up to 32 total passengers).
Weekday (Monday-Thursday) Specials:
10% off your charter of 8 or more people, or Military and Public Safety 10% discount, or Children under 12 are $50 once minimum base fare of $650 is met.
